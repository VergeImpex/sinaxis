Department of Power Electronics

The Power Electronics department at Sinaxis has been a pioneer in developing, repairing, and indigenizing advanced power systems since 2004. Over the years, the department has executed significant projects for the Indian Navy, Air Force, and other major defense organizations. With expertise in high-voltage systems, modular repairs, and advanced indigenization, the department has delivered over 600 units across various domains.

What services does Sinaxis provide?
Sinaxis offers comprehensive services in Power Electronics and Radio Frequencies, including custom design, development, indigenization, and repairs of high-performance modules and systems. We also specialize in testing and quality assurance for mission-critical applications.
Can Sinaxis develop custom solutions for specific requirements?
Absolutely! Our teams excel in creating bespoke solutions tailored to unique client needs, whether it’s designing advanced power modules, indigenizing obsolete components, or developing state-of-the-art RF systems.
What industries does Sinaxis cater to?
We primarily serve the defense, aerospace, and industrial sectors, providing robust, reliable solutions for demanding environments and complex operational requirements.
Does Sinaxis offer product repair and testing services?
Yes, we provide complete repair and testing services, ensuring all components and systems function optimally. Our testing process includes environmental and performance validation for real-world applications.
